Life inspiration

Sunday, 29 Mar 2020
Photo by Ken Cheung on Unsplash

Life is not only about work. It is not even about having fun all the time: try eating day after day the same dish and sooner more than later you will end up hating it. Motivation is what sits behind the curtrains and pulls the strings of our daily actions. However motivation is like a fragile plant that needs daily care. This page is a small collection of different resources, helpful tids and bits to refresh motivation and perhaps provide a different perspective on daily life. Sometimes we cannot change what happens, and yet we always have control on how we see it.
